Forest Bath

Forest Bath is a free, fun, and healthy space for people of all ages to enjoy the outdoors and connect with nature. Located in the Pokhara, Nepal area, Forest Bath is managed and organized by youth who are passionate about swimming, the environment, and community development. Forest Bath envisions a place where people can come together to relax, have fun, and learn about each other's cultures. We want it to be a space where people of all ages can feel safe and welcome, and where they can enjoy the beauty of nature. We believe that Forest Bath can play a valuable role in the community by providing a healthy and affordable alternative to traditional swimming pools. We also believe that it can be a catalyst for social change by bringing people together from different backgrounds and cultures. We are committed to making Forest Bath a reality, and we are excited to see what the future holds for this project.

Forest Bath is located in a beautiful forest near Pame, Pokhara, Nepal. The forest is home to a variety of trees, plants, and animals, and it offers stunning views of the Annapurna mountains. Forest Bath is a free, fun, and healthy space for people of all ages to enjoy the outdoors and connect with nature. The location is easily accessible by car or bus, and there is plenty of parking available. The forest is also close to several other popular tourist destinations, such as Phewa Lake, Begnas Lake, and the Devi's Fall. Forest Bath is managed by a team of young people from Nepal who are passionate about swimming, the environment, and community development. The team is committed to providing a safe, fun, and welcoming environment for all visitors.
